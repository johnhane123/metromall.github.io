ABOUT US:

The mall was built in 2022 and is owned by AALS Company. Since its establishment, it has undergone several renovations and expansions, with the addition of new stores, dining options, and entertainment facilities. Over the years, it has become a popular destination for both locals and tourists, and continues to be a hub for shopping, dining, and entertainment.

MISSION:

To provide our customers with a world-class shopping and entertainment experience by offering a diverse range of products and services, exceptional customer service, and a welcoming atmosphere that encourages community engagement.

VISSION:

To be recognized as the premier shopping and entertainment destination in the region by continually improving our facilities, enhancing our offerings, and prioritizing customer satisfaction. We aim to be an integral part of the community, creating long-term partnerships with local businesses and organizations and supporting the growth and development of the area.

DESCRIPTION:


The mall caters to a diverse target market, with offerings and experiences designed for people of all ages and interests. Families with children can enjoy a range of entertainment options and kid-friendly activities, while fashion enthusiasts can explore a wide selection of trendy clothing and accessories. Foodies can indulge in a variety of culinary delights from around the world, and tech-savvy shoppers can explore the latest gadgets and electronics. The mall also attracts tourists who are looking for a unique shopping experience, as well as local residents who enjoy the convenience of having a wide range of stores and services all in one location.
